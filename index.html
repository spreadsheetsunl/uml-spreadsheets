UML Spreadsheets

UML Spreadsheets is a UML-based visual language that provides a controlled specification and generation of spreadsheets, covering the most common spreadsheets patterns, and that is compatible with the most widely used spreadsheet applications. The spreadsheets generated from this domain-specific language will guide the users so they make less mistakes, based on the restrictions imposed in the modeling phase.

Paper

The paper On the Emergence of Patterns for Spreadsheets Data Arrangements describes UML Spreadsheets.
